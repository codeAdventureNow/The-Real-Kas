Explore Kas Beaches

Kas is a laid-back seaside town nestled along the Mediterranean coastline of southwestern Turkiye. You can explore ancient ruins, sail on guided boat trips to nearby islands, and experience world-renowned scuba diving.

The areas around Kas boast some of the most attractive beaches in the Mediterranean sea. We will introduce some of the top beaches in the Kas area that you should consider visiting on your next getaway.

Little Pebble Beach

Little Pebble Beach can be reached within a few minutes of old town Kas. This small beach cove packs extensive views of nearby islands and lush turquoise water. Sunbeds and umbrellas hug the beach line while a few small eateries are ready to serve your favorite frosty beverage while soaking up the sun and the views. The water in the summertime is moderate temp, just right in my opinion. Consider bringing water shoes if you have sensitive feet like me.

Patara Beach

Ready to get three Instagram-worthy locations in one location? This spot checks several boxes to make you look like an exotic world traveler. Rolling sand dunes. Check! Miles of stunning coastline. Check! Historic ruins in the ancient city of Patara! Check again!

With 12km of sandy beach and a broad 500-meter width, you will have plenty of space to relax and spread your tanned wings. This beach is a place to escape the crowds and tap into your beach bliss.

Kaputas Beach

Kaputas is the most popular beach destination in the region. And for a good reason. It is stunning! A gorgeous oasis awaits you at the bottom of a steep cliff line. This beach is for fun and, of course, magnificent views. With the water, a little bit wavy many of the visitors glide up and down with the ocean tide. Get here early to catch a sunbed and to find a parking spot along the highway. In the summertime, expect the beach to be packed to the brim.
